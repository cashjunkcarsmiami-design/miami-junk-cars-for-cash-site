Junk Car Pickup Checklist

Before a tow truck arrives, remove personal property, collect the keys and available paperwork, and make sure the vehicle can be reached safely. Tell the buyer if the car does not roll, steer, or have all four wheels.

  • Remove documents, toll transponders, plates, tools, and personal belongings.
  • Have the title, registration, identification, keys, and VIN ready when available.
  • Share accurate details about damage, missing parts, locked wheels, or flat tires.
  • Clear other cars, gates, and obstacles from the pickup area.
  • Ask the property owner or manager for access when the car is in a garage or private lot.

Why Access Details Matter

A vehicle in a narrow driveway, parking garage, apartment complex, mechanic shop, or storage yard may need special planning. Photos and exact location details help the towing team bring suitable equipment and avoid delays.
